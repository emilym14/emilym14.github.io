Introduction

, I am a multimedia journalism and creative writing double major at Virginia Tech; now in my final year of college, I continue to seek out new methods of telling stories. Continually, I look to incorporate new mediums into the experience: photographs, film, audio, 360-degree video and interactive graphics.
My role as a creative storyteller is to combine the knowledge I have of both technical journalism and boundary-pushing creative writing to construct innovative content for publications, to think outside the box and to bring multiple dimensions to the content I create, specifically within this course of Writing and Digital Media.

Interests

A little about me—I am an active and passionate storyteller, an avid coffee drinker, anti-Oxford comma and a student who fully embraces her multimedia journalist title. As one may presume, I love to write. In addition to writing, I foster a passion for film, as well as photography. I like to utilize those passions in a way that allows me to contribute to greater causes, to get involved with programs that work to help others and spread the love and hope of Jesus Christ.
