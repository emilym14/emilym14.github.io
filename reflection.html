Exploring the VT-Shaped Student

The T-Shaped Student: A Podcast

What is this podcast about?

The T-shaped podcast was the second project assigned to us this semester in writing and digital media. For this assignment I worked in a group with two classmates to create an original play that served to illustrate the T-shaped student as we understood it. The vertical base of the T represents an established line of experience in one area, whereas the shorter, horizontal line on top of the T represents an interest in outside, complimentary areas of work and study.

In this podcast, we each contributed to the writing of the script and starred as one of the main characters in the storyline. This story is about Billy, a freshman engineering student who struggles when given an essay assignment; in order to improve, he seeks out help at the on-campus writing center and discovers he has an interest in English after all.

The V-Shaped Student: A Video

What is this video about?

The V-shaped video was the third project assigned to us this semester in writing and digital media. For this assignment I worked in the same group as I did for the previous assignment, the T-shaped podcast. My group created a video that served to depict our understanding of the V-shaped student; the V-shaped student is a student who invests in life outside the classroom at Virginia Tech in addition to his or her academia. This is demonstrated in a number of ways: internships, study abroad programs, research, organizations, clubs and more.

In our video, we each contributed to the writing of the script and starred as one of the main characters in the storyline. This story is about Jimmy, a freshman English student who is struggling to understand the internship-hunting process, as well as what it means to get involved outside of classes.

Here, Jimmy is encouraged by his roommate and a Red Cross recruiter to build a resume, dress the part and explore his passions and interests outside the classroom.
